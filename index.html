Circuit Rules

Welcome to today's Circuit.

To compete, your goal is to find the day's target in as few guesses as possible.

Press and hold the screen to expand your guess, and release to see how close it is to the target.

You are always allowed to see your last two guesses, and the closer you are to the target, the greener your guess will appear.

The target is reset at Midnight EST every night.

Good luck!
