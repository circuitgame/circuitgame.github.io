Circuit Rules

Welcome to today's Circuit.

Your goal is to find the size of the day's target circle in as few guesses as possible.

Press and hold the screen to expand your guess's size, and release to see how close it is to the target size.

You are always allowed to see your last two guesses, and the closer you are to the target, the greener your guess will appear. The more red your guess is, the farther you are form the target's size.

The target is reset at Midnight EST every night.

Good luck!
